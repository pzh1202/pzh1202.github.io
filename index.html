Xuelian Cheng

I am currently a PhD student in Monash University, working with A/Prof. Zongyuan Ge, Dr. Mehrtash Harandi, Prof. Tom Drummond.

My research interests include visual geometry and low-level vision tasks, i.e., depth estimation; AutoML, i.e., Neural Architecture Search (NAS); medical imaging and open-set scenarios.
